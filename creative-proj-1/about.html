OUR STORY

Sun Statement was founded by two sisters from Morgan. Utah. In high school and college, these sisters found their own individual unique taste for fashion, jewelry, and accessories. They came together and meshed their styles to create timeless jewelry pieces that will elevate any everyday outfit.

The sisters had dreamed of bringing their ideas to life. With the support of family and friends, they launched Sun Statement Jewelry in 2022.

Our pieces are made with the highest quality material to stand the test of time. We are excited to help others feel confident and beautiful with the jewelry they wear.
